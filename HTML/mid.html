Mid Lane

Mid lane is the center point on the map. Playing in this lane will require you to have above average map awareness since your lane is so central to the map you can expect a lot of ganking and attention from other lanes. Since mid lane has two entrances to the lane on either side you won't be able to ward and have vision on both sides of at once. To compensate for this you will have to choose which side you want to wait and stick as much to that side of the lane as you can. By doing so you will force possible ganks to take the long route towards you giving you time to react while having vision on your side preventing ganks entirely as long as your ware enough.

As for mid lane your goal should be to snowball yourself and the game for an easy win. The best way you can do this is by roaming to the other lanes and ganking them resulting in possible kills and objectives. Do note that roaming at the wrong time can be devastating to you and put you behind more than any other thing in the game besides maybe dying. In the later parts of the game you will be responsible for dealing as much damage as you can for your team while remaining safe. The only exception to this are ad casters which we will come back on a little later. If you do decide to pick an ad caster do keep in mind your team need to have at least one ap damage champion in the team or you will most likely lose the game.

In mid there is usually a choice between two types of champions mages and ad casters. Mages excel in loads of damage while remaining from a safe distance. They often have skill shots that with high damage meant to harass the enemy and whittle their opponent down. At later levels mages tend to have high amounts of burst that can kill an enemy champion with one skill rotation. Ad casters on the other hand excel at bursting their opponent down. As an ad caster you want your opponent to be around the middle of the lane so when you do engage and burst them down you have the opportunity to chase after them if they survive. Ad caster are almost always melee champions which puts you at a disadvantage in the early game against mages. The play style of an ad caster is to get in kill a high propriety target and get out before they die themselves.

For mid lane the most picked spells are flash and ignite. Flash offers great offensive and defensive capabilities by giving you a shirt range blink while ignite gives you more damage in a fight. If you expect to struggle with keeping yourself alive you might want to opt for barrier which gives you a short lasting shield that can help you survive high bursts of damage.

Mid Lane Champions

Ahri

A vastaya who can reshape magic into orbs of raw energy Ahri charms her victims before stealing their life force away. Ahri can send out her orb or raw energy damaging all it touches. If caught in close quarters combat surround herself in fox fire that homes in on the closest target. Her ultimate gives her the ability to dash forward three times sending out fiery bolts each time dashes.

Akali

Abandoning the Kinkou Order and her title of the Fist of Shadow Akali now works alone to defend Ilonia from its enemies. When in combat Akali can drop a shroud making her completely invisible from the darkness she strikes throwing shuriken at her opponent. For her ultimate she dashes in stunning the target. After a brief period she will dash again this time executing the target.

Anivia

A Freljord demigod over the circle of life, death and rebirth Anivia protects the people of the north who see her as a symbol of hope. Anivia can create walls of ice cornering her enemies and leaving them unable to run away before she bombards them with shards of ice. Her ultimate lets her conjure up a snowstorm that slowly grows bigger over time. Enemies caught in the storm will be slowed and take damage.

Annie

A child mage with immense pyromantic power Annie has yet to learn to fully control her power burning her surroundings based on her emotions. With her strong control over fire Annie is able to hurl fireballs at her enemies and incinerate them in a cone of fire whenever they get to close. Her ultimate lets her summon a giant fiery shadow bear named Tibers who will fight for her and protect her.

Aurelion Sol

A god that once graced the vast emptiness of the cosmos with celestial wonders of his own devising. He was tricked by an interstellar empire into their servitude. Aurelion Soul uses the power of the stars that surround him to obliterate all of his opponents. His ultimate has him blasting a beam of pure starlight that deals tons of damage and knocks back any enemies caught in the blast.

Azir

Once the great emperor of Shurima Azir was betrayed and killed. Now centuries later he has been resurrected as an ascended being rising his buried kingdom from the sands. Being the emperor of Shurima Azir conjures sand soldiers to fight for him. He can command these soldiers to charge into battle and kill anything he commands. His ultimate sends out a wave of shielded sand soldiers pushing enemies back and preventing enemies from passing.

Brand

Once a tribesman of the icy Freljord named Kegan Rodhe Brand now no more than a vessel of living flames. Being the vessel of living flame brand has the power to conjure up great fire pillars setting his enemies ablaze dealing damage over time. Blasting them with fire so strong it spreads to nearby enemies. His ultimate sends out a massive blast of fire dealing damage and bouncing between enemies up to five times.

Cassiopeia

A viper like predator Cassiopeia once was the daughter of a noble Noxian household before she went into a tomb in search of ancient power where she got bitten by a tomb guarding transforming her. Cassiopeia uses a range of venoms and toxins to slow and damage her enemies. Her ultimate will petrify anybody who looks her directly in the eyes while slowing those who are lucky enough to miss her gaze.

Ekko

A prodigy from the rough streets of Zaun Ekko has crafted a device that gives him control over time allowing him to achieve the impossible. When fighting Ekko can split the time's lines slowing enemies who are caught in it. He can then rebalance the split granting him a shield and stunning them. His ultimate allows him to move back in time healing himself and moving back to the position he originally was.

Fizz

An amphibious yordle Fizz dwells around among the reefs near Bilgewater often returning the tithes cast into the sea. Fizz fights using his mighty trident he dashes into combat damaging enemies before hopping into the air using his trident as a pole for him to stand on confusing his enemies before slipping away. His ultimate tosses a small fish onto his enemies. After a few seconds a massive shark erupts eating the fish and his enemy.

Galio

Outside the city of Demacia Gallios stands watch built as a bulwark against enemy mages he is highly resistant to magic. Gallio uses his strong stone wings to send forward two gusts of wind that will converge into a tornado slowing enemies before charging into combat. He can assume a defensive stance greatly increasing his defenses. His ultimate allows him to jump into the air before crashing down on his enemies knock them up.

Heimerdinger

Brilliant yet eccentric yordle scientist Heimerdinger is the best inventor Piltover has ever seen. He is obsessed with his work and thrives on finding answers. When forced to fight Heimerdinger relies on gadgets to do the fighting for him. He builds turrets that will shoot down his opponent while throwing grenades that will stun them. His ultimate allows him to upgraded one of his abilities making it much stronger.

Karthus

The harbinger of oblivion, Karthus is an undying spirit whose haunting songs are a prelude to the horror of his nightmarish appearance. When fighting Karthus uses power soul explosion to blow up is foes while surrounding himself in the souls of the falls who quickly eat away at the foes life. His ultimate calls forward a massive beam of light from the skies dealing tons of damage to everybody no matter where they are.

Kassadin

A Shuriman guide while lost his village and family to the void Kassadin seeks revenge by killing the self-proclaimed prophet of the void Malzhar. Kassadin wields the blade of Horok dealing bonus magic damage on every strike. He is able to use void energy to stop channeling spells while at the same time he absorbs strength from spells cast in his vicinity. His ultimate lets him teleport dealing damage at the location he appears.

Katarina

Decisive in judgment and lethal in combat, Katarina is a Noxian assassin of the highest caliber. Katarina fights using daggers and throwing knives. She can throw a dagger at an enemy so that she can teleport towards it and slash around her dealing damage. She can also teleport to allies to get out of harm's way. Her ultimate lets her spin around throwing out a flurry of blades hitting the nearest three enemies.

LeBlanc

mysterious even to other members of the Black Rose cabal, LeBlanc has manipulated events since the early days of Noxius. Le Blanc uses her powerful magic to burst down a single enemy in combat. Silencing the enemy so they can not retaliate and shackling them with magic there is no change her opponent will ever get away. Her ultimate lets her clone her last used ability for a more powerful version.

Lissandra

As the reclusive leader of the Frostguard, many believe Lissandra is a living saint whose followers bring healing and wisdom but nothing is less true. Lissandra uses her elemental damage to wield the power of true ice entombing or impaling all who oppose her. Her ultimate ability lets her entomb herself or an enemy in true ice. When cast upon herself she will heal otherwise it will damage her opponent.

Lux

A powerful mage that wields light Lux comes from a noble Demacian family forced to keep her powers a secret to protects her family status. Wielding the power of light Lux is able to ensnare her enemies following up with a devastating explosion of light. She can also use her powers to shield her enemies from harm. Her ultimate lets her shoot a massive beam of pure light dealing tons of damage to her enemies.

Malzahar

A zealous seer dedicated to the unification of all life, Malzahar truly believes the newly emergent Void to be the path to Runeterra’s salvation. Malzahar uses his void powers to summon little voidlings that will attack his enemies. Tearing two rifts in the void Malzahar damages and silences anything in-between. His ultimate channels the essence of the void suppressing the target in place damaging them.

Orianna

Once a curious girl of flesh and blood Orianna is now a technological marvel comprised entirely of clockwork. Oriana uses her companion ball to throw at enemies dealing damage as allowing her to release energy causing even more damage. She can also command the ball onto an ally shielding them. Her ultimate causes a massive shockwave that pulls all enemies caught in it into its center.

Ryze

Widely considered one of the most adept sorcerers on Runeterra, Ryze is an ancient, hard-bitten archmage with an impossibly heavy burden to bear. Ryze uses his powerful magic to trap his enemies in a cage of runes stunning them. He throws bolts of energy pure energy dealing damage as well as cursing his targets. His ultimate allows him to teleport him and his allies to a new location after a short delay.
